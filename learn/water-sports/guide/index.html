Water Skiing

Getting Started

Starting Position

  1. Keep skis parallel
  2. Arms straight
  3. Knees bent
  4. Let the boat do the work

Start with combo skis for easier learning

Deep Water Start

  • Skis pointed up
  • Handle at hip level
  • Chin up, back straight

Basic Form

  • Weight centered
  • Shoulders level
  • Arms relaxed

Personal Protection

  • Always wear a life jacket
  • Use appropriate safety gear

Awareness

  • Watch for other boats
  • Monitor weather conditions
  • Keep safe distances
