Casting Techniques

Overhead Cast

  1. Position rod at 2 o'clock
  2. Smooth back motion
  3. Pause at top
  4. Forward cast to target

Pro Tips:

  • Keep wrist firm
  • Watch back cast
  • Stop rod at 10 o'clock

Side Cast

  1. Rod parallel to ground
  2. Sweep back smoothly
  3. Power through cast
  4. Follow through
