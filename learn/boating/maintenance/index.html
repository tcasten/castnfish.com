Engine Maintenance

Oil Changes

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Warm up engine (5-10 minutes)
  2. Turn off and drain old oil
  3. Replace oil filter
  4. Add new marine-grade oil
  5. Check oil level

You'll Need:

  • Marine-grade oil
  • Oil filter
  • Filter wrench
  • Oil pan
  • Rags

Fuel System

Always use fuel stabilizer when storing boat for extended periods.

Regular Checks:

  • Inspect fuel lines for cracks
  • Check fuel filter condition
  • Test fuel pump operation
  • Look for water in fuel

Hull Care

Cleaning

Cleaning Schedule:

  • Rinse after each use
  • Deep clean monthly
  • Wax every 3-4 months

Bottom Paint

When to Repaint:

  • Every 1-2 years
  • When paint is flaking
  • Before heavy growth appears

Common Issues & Solutions

Engine Won't Start

  1. Check fuel level and connections
  2. Verify battery connections
  3. Test ignition switch
  4. Check kill switch position
Watch Diagnostic Video

Poor Performance

  1. Check propeller condition
  2. Inspect fuel filter
  3. Clean/replace spark plugs
  4. Verify trim settings

Maintenance Schedule

Regular Maintenance Calendar

After Each Use

  • Flush engine with fresh water
  • Wipe down surfaces
  • Check fluid levels

Monthly

  • Check battery condition
  • Inspect belts and hoses
  • Test navigation lights

Seasonally

  • Change oil and filter
  • Service power steering
  • Check/replace anodes

Annually

  • Full engine service
  • Hull inspection
  • Replace water pump
