Safety Essentials

Life Jackets (PFDs)

Every passenger must have access to a properly fitting life jacket. Children under 13 must wear them at all times.

Weather Safety

Before You Go

  • Check local weather forecast
  • Monitor marine weather radio
  • Understand weather warning flags
  • Know your boat's limitations

Warning Signs

Approaching Storms

  • Dark, threatening clouds
  • Sudden wind shifts
  • Temperature drops
  • Lightning on horizon

Emergency Procedures

Man Overboard

  1. Shout "Man overboard!" and maintain visual contact
  2. Throw flotation device
  3. Turn boat around following proper procedure
  4. Approach person from downwind
